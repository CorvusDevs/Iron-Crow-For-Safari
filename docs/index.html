Iron Crow

A polished ad blocker for Safari on macOS, iPhone, and iPad. Auto-updating filter lists, per-site control, and a clean dashboard.

Auto-updating lists

Filter lists refresh on their own in the background, so blocking stays current without app updates.

Per-site control

Turn blocking on or off for any site from the Safari toolbar, with a clear view of what was hidden.

Built-in lists remove cookie-consent pop-ups and other page clutter, not just ads.

Iron Crow does not track you or collect your browsing. A one-time purchase, no subscriptions.

Fast and native

Built on Safari's native content blocking, so pages load quickly and battery stays happy.
