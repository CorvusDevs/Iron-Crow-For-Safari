Privacy Policy

Iron Crow is built to protect your privacy, so it collects as little as possible.

What we collect

Nothing. Iron Crow does not collect, store, or transmit your browsing history, the sites you visit, or any personal information. There are no analytics and no tracking.

Where your settings live

Your filter-list choices, per-site rules, and custom filters are stored only on your device. They never leave it.

Filter lists

To block ads, the app downloads public filter lists (such as EasyList) directly from their providers. These are plain rule files. Your identity is not attached to those requests beyond the normal network information any download involves.

Payments

Iron Crow is a one-time purchase handled entirely by the App Store. We never see your payment details.
